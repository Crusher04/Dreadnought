

Here is your list of keywords that can be used to navigate the menu
and to navigate throughout the game. These keywords are words that
are generally not displayed on the screen to type as a command.


Keywords
---------

'Quit' or 'Exit'
	Either of these keywords will immediately exit and stop the game from
	running.

'Menu'
	This keyword will return you back to the main menu.

'Back'
	This keyword will return you to the previous screen.
